§ Ingredient notes

Whole grain corn

Provides fiber and whole-grain carbohydrates — a positive on an otherwise refined cereal.

Added sugars

Sugar and corn syrup drive ~36% of the product by weight. Cane sugar and high-fructose corn syrup have similar metabolic impact despite different marketing — total quantity matters most.

Artificial colors

Red 40, Yellow 5, and Blue 1 are synthetic dyes. Some research links them to behavioral effects in children. The EU requires warning labels on products containing them.

§ 02 — what it does

01 · Scan

Read any nutrition label, instantly.

Point your phone at the back of any package. Nouri's AI decodes the ingredient list, macronutrients, and the fine print you'd normally skip — and tells you what actually matters.

02 · Personal

Filtered through your profile.

Tell Nouri about your allergies and food preferences once. Every scan is scored against your profile — vegan, halal, gluten-free, tree nuts, low sugar, whatever matters to you.

03 · Pantry

A pantry that remembers.

Every product you scan stays on file. Set expiry dates, track what's still good, never forget the jar at the back of the cupboard again.Plus

From scan to answer in five seconds.

No accounts to manage, no calorie counting, no guilt. Just clarity.

§ 03 — how it works

  1. i.

    Set your profile, once.

    Tap the allergens and diets that apply to you. It takes about a minute. You can edit it anytime.

    ~60s
  2. ii.

    Scan the back of the package.

    Point your camera at the nutrition panel. The AI reads the ingredients and macros — you don't need a barcode.

    ~4s
  3. iii.

    Read the real story.

    Nouri summarizes the label in plain English, flags anything you're allergic to, and tells you whether it fits the way you eat.

    done
